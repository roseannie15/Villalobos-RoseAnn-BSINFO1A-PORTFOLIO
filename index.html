About Me

I am Rose Ann Villalobos, an IT student at Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU). My interest in information technology started through my experiences with applications, games, and the dramas and vlogs I usually watch. These inspired me to learn how applications are created and how they can still be improved.


I am particularly interested in making applications more user-friendly and visually appealing. For example, when I use applications for reading manhwa, I often notice features and designs that could be enhanced to give users a better experience.


During my senior high school years, I chose the ICT strand, where I discovered that I truly enjoy working on technical projects. Although some tasks are challenging, I find satisfaction in fixing errors and seeing my programs run smoothly.

Education: BS in Information Technology (LSPU)
Location: Philippines
Interests: Web Design, UI/UX, Technical Projects
